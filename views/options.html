Jammed
Many of you have been asking about the difference between a jam and a fruit butter. Jams require that you cook the fruit at a higher temp with a lot of sugar. Fruit bitters are cooked slowly (usually in a crockpot for about 10 hours) to bring out the natural sugars of the fruit. Very little sugar is added.
Jam and butter flavors are seasonal.
